We are a wholesale coffee bean company that is striving to bring ethically sourced and carefully chosen coffee beans to consumers. We want to do this in the least expensive way possible, without compromising on quality, so that no matter what level of experience you have with coffee, you can brew at the highest level.
We are a small,new company, but we are always available to give you any advice on how to pick out the best beans for you, hone your brewing skills, and help you discover the bounds of your palate. We also hope to be able to offer introductory classes on cupping and brewing in the near future as we expand. Thank you for supporting our business!
